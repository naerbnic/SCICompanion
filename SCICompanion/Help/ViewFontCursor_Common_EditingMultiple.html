Editing multiple cels

View and font resources have multiple cels to work on. By selecting Affect all cels in loop or Affect all characters in the left-hand pane, the drawing functions will operate on multiple cels at once.

This functionality is very useful for quickly editing all cels in a view animations. For example, you can change the colour of a character's clothing for an entire animation with just a few clicks.

Notes

  • The canvas background will turn red to remind you your actions are affecting all cels.
  • Most drawing tools operate on a specific part of the image. To calculate the appropriate location of the other cels in a view loop, SCI Companion uses the offset properties of the cels.
