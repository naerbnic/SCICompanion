Output PaneÂ¶

The output pane in SCICompanion appears when you compile, do a project-wide search or debug a game. It consists of three tabs: Compiler, Find Results and Debug Output.

You can double-click on compile messages and find results to take you directly to the problem or search result.

You may close it at anytime by clicking on the X.
