Selection and copy/paste

Selecting an area of the canvas.

To select an area of the canvas, choose the selection tool and left-click and drag on the area you wish to select. You can copy (CTRL-C) or cut (CTRL-X) the selection to the clipboard. You can also drag the selected area around to move it.

Paste

If you paste (CTRL-V) an image from the clipboard into the view, it will appear in the top left of the current cel, in a rubber band selection rectangle. You can position it as you desired. Double-clicking on the selection will set it permanently.

Transparent paste

Transparent pasting (CTRL-SHIFT-V) will paste an image, but not include the pixels that are the same colour as the transparent colour of the cel

Selecting the whole canvas

The entire canvas may be selected by pressing CTRL-A or choosing Select All from the Edit menu.
