Vocab editor

SCI uses vocab resources for various things. The only one you can edit is the one that contains the words the SCI parser understands.

The words are listed in the view, along with their Group (not significant for editing) and Class (the type of word is it). Synonyms are grouped together on one line.

Topics

  • Adding and deleting words.
  • Looking up a word.
  • Editing words and adding synonyms.
  • Specifying the word class.

Note

Words can also be added directly from the script editor.
