Adding or removing cels and loops

Adding a loop or cel

In the left-hand pane, there are two buttons each for loops and cels: Add before and Add after. These will add a new loop or cel after or before the currently selected one.

By default, a newly added cel will be a copy of the currently selected one. In the Preferences dialog, this can be changed so that instead, new cels will just be filled with the current cel's transparent colour.

Removing a cel

Click on the cel in bottom pane, and press DELETE

Removing a loop

By removing all cels in a loop, the loop will be removed.
